One of the biggest porn accounts HACKED
2024-12-8 LHT News
The LazaGrad Hack team managed to target one of the largest accounts on the pornographic website Postimg and shut it down completely. The operation revealed around 1,200 inappropriate images stored within the account, which were accessed and examined before the decision was made to permanently close the account.
Decision to permanently close the account. This account was a major source of unethical publication in France, and its targeting came as part of our ongoing efforts to counter such activities on the Internet. The process included:
Full access to the account using a vulnerability discovered in the Postimg platform. Delete and distort the content to prevent photos from being reused or restored. Closing the account permanently to ensure that it does not return to work again.
We consider this process as a warning to the platforms that host this kind of content without real censorship. Websites must review their security policies urgently, because teams such as Lazagrad Hack are able to detect and target any platform that does not comply with safety and ethics standards.
The message is clear: No content that violates values or uses the Internet to publish what is inappropriate will not be tolerated. We continue to target accounts and platforms that ignore their ethical responsibility.
