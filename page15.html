Accounts of e-people.gov.tn HACKED
2024-12-15 LHT News
The Lazagrad Hack team managed to hack a number of personal accounts on the Tunisian government website, e-people.gov.tn, dedicated to providing electronic services to citizens.
The hack came as a direct reaction to the spread of prostitution in Tunisia without any punishment or legal accountability. The operation showed the weak security structure of the government electronic system, and the team was able to access sensitive data for registered users.
This attack highlights the lack of adequate security oversight in government institutions, and confirms that we are able to target any platform that is not committed to protecting the data of its citizens.
