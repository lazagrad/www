Flingster accounts HACKED
2024-12-10 LHT News
The Lazagrad Hack team managed to carry out a wide hacking operation that targeted many accounts on the Flingster platform, one of the largest platforms targeting young people in Russia and promoting corruption.
The process included full access to the accounts, and after hacking, all accounts linked to the site were permanently deleted, disrupting the platform's activity and stopping its negative impact.
This hack aims to confront the platforms that contribute to the spread of corruption and the exploitation of youth, and emphasizes our team's commitment to confronting such activities on the Internet..
