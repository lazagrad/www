Kurdish Qeep Manager Account HACKED
2024-1-11 LHT News
In a dangerous development that reflects the escalation of cyber attacks, the Lazagrad Hack team announced the implementation of a wide penetration operation targeting the Qeep platform, one of the most prominent dating platforms in the Kurdistan region. The process, described as complex and organized, resulted in full control over the site manager's account and progressive incursion until reaching the home page and comprehensive control of the platform.
Accurate details about the operation The hacking process began by exploiting internal security vulnerabilities, the details of which have not yet been revealed. The process took several stages, in which the Lazagrad Hack team cleverly included security systems until it imposed full control over the infrastructure of the site.
The direct impacts on the QEEP platform had a very dangerous impact on the platform, and the most prominent results included: Disabled access to the platform for intermittent periods as a result of server control. Possible leakage of sensitive users and management data. Weakening confidence in the security of the platform, which may prompt users to abandon its use. Sending direct messages to users bearing the hacked team signature, causing anxiety and confusion.
Causes and motives for the attack Although no official statement was issued explaining the direct causes of the operation, estimates indicate several possibilities: exposing the security gaps at the site and drawing attention to the weak protection systems. A warning message to the management of the platform about the danger of neglecting the security aspects. Demonstrate the cyber power of the Lazagrad Hack team and confirm their control over highly complex systems. Click on QEEP management to improve user data protection system. The position of the QEEP administration and the reactions so far, the management of the QEEP platform has not issued any official statement regarding this attack, which raises many questions about its ability to manage the crisis. But the administration is expected to start with urgent steps, including: a comprehensive investigation to determine how the breach occurred and fill the gaps. Strengthening protection systems and developing the site structure to prevent the recurrence of such accidents. Notify users of any potential impact on their personal data.
