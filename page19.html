Mustafa Maroufin's account HACKED
2024-12-20 LHT News
In a hacking operation, the details of which will be announced soon, the Lazagrad Hack team managed to hack the account of Moroccan poet Mostafa Maaroufi on the French social networking platform Diaspora-FR.
Who is Mostafa Maaroufi? Mostafa Maaroufi, a Moroccan poet and writer, was born in 1960. He published his poems and articles in Arab newspapers and magazines, and some of his poems were broadcast on the waves of local radio stations. He has a collection of collections on the Internet, and is considered one of the prominent poetic voices in Morocco.
What is the Diaspora-FR platform? Diaspora-FR is part of the Diaspora* network, a non-centre-owned social network that allows them to fully control their data and content. The network consists of a group of independent servers (known as "pods") that communicate with each other to form an integrated social network.
Penetration details: Goal: Poet Mostafa Maaroufi's account on Diaspora-FR. The result: the account has been hacked and the data related to it is kept.
