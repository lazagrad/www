5 million riyals, Iranserver HACKED
2024-12-26 LHT News
In a new step in the campaign supporting the Syrian brothers, the LazaGrad Hack group announced the implementation of a qualitative hack targeting a paid Iranian account on the Iranian hosting platform IranServer. The group confirmed that the account contains a balance of 5 million Iranian riyals, which reflects a new development in the cyber attack strategies implemented by the group to support Arab and Islamic causes.
This breakthrough came as part of a series of operations aimed at weakening the digital structure of the parties that are considered hostile to the issues of the Arab peoples, especially the Syrian issue. IranServer is one of Iran's most prominent e-hosting services, making it a powerful message that shows the extent of its digital defenses.
Operation Details: Objective: A paid account of 5 million Iranian riyals. Target Platform: IranServer, one of the major web hosting platforms in Iran. The message: Supporting the Syrian brothers by weakening the digital infrastructure of the systems that support repression in the region.
The political message behind the hack The LazaGrad Hack group confirms that this operation comes within a series of cyber attacks aimed at responding to the regimes that support the aggression against the Arab peoples, especially the Syrian people. The group believes that Iran, through its direct support for the Syrian regime, has become a direct target of its cyber campaigns. The campaign continues This hack comes as part of a long series of operations carried out by the group over the past weeks, which targeted server sites and digital systems in various countries. The group confirmed its determination to continue these attacks until it achieves its goals of supporting the Syrian revolution and weakening oppressive regimes in the region.
