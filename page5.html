Israeli company Hot Mobile's database HACKED
2024-11-27 LHT News
The LazaGrad Hack team managed to hack a huge database of Hot Mobile, where 3,248,000 records with a total size of 1.6 GB were accessed.
The data obtained include: phone numbers, full names, locations (addresses), servers DNS, identifiers, personal domains.
