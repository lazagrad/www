Microsoft for Israeli IDF HACKED
2024-12-9 LHT News
The Lazagrad Hack team managed to hack a group of Microsoft accounts belonging to the Israeli Ministry of Defense. The operation targeted the official accounts that are used in the management of military business and internal communication, and the team was able to temporarily disable these accounts.
The target site is part of a technical network that aims to develop cyber solutions to support the regimes of the Zionist entity, making it a direct target for our operations. The team was able to exploit the security holes within the site's systems to reach its main servers without any significant resistance, which indicates the weakness of the security structure of this platform, which is supposed to be specialized in cybersecurity.
The attack took advantage of the security measures associated with the password management, which led to the suspension of accounts so that the ministry could recover them through complex recovery procedures.
This penetration comes within the framework of a series of operations targeting the Zionist regimes and infrastructure, and once again proves that even the major security institutions are not immune to the security gaps.
